### Description
Transform your events with TicketFloLIVE - the complete mobile solution for event organizers. Seamlessly manage ticket sales, check-in attendees, and process payments all from your iPhone.

**Key Features:**
• Real-time ticket scanning and validation
• Professional Point of Sale system for merchandise
• Apple Wallet integration for contactless tickets
• Tap to Pay on iPhone for seamless transactions
• Live analytics and attendee management
• Instant QR code generation and verification
• Secure payment processing with Stripe

**Perfect for:**
• Event organizers and venue managers
• Conference and festival coordinators
• Concert and theater venues
• Corporate event planners
• Small business events

**Professional Tools:**
• Complete inventory management
• Real-time sales tracking
• Customer information management
• Revenue analytics and reporting
• Multi-event support

TicketFloLIVE integrates with the TicketFlo web platform, giving you powerful event management tools both online and on-the-go. Accept payments, scan tickets, and manage your events with professional-grade tools designed for modern event organizers.
